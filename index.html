Our Story

Founded in 2015, KRP Salon began as a small boutique studio with a vision to redefine beauty standards. Today, we've grown into a premier destination for those seeking exceptional service in a welcoming environment.

Our team of certified professionals undergoes continuous training to stay at the forefront of industry trends and techniques. We're committed to using only the highest quality, cruelty-free products.
